About
Technoscape

TechnoScape is the biggest annual technology event held by Binus Computer Club (BNCC), where participants can learn with experts from the fields to widen their horizons, share ideas along with innovation, and solve real-life problems. TechnoScape has been held annually since 2018. Since then, it has been successful in providing information and education on the latest technology trends. This year, TechnoScape consists of three interesting events, namely Virtual Conference, Developer Workshop, and Hackathon 4.0 brings insightful information for participants.
