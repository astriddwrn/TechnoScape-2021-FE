About Hackathon 4.0
Hackathon 4.0 is a 48-hour online coding competition where each team competes to create innovative applications or websites that could solve problems. This year is the 4th time Binus Computer Club (BNCC) held a Hackathon event. Throughout the years, Hackathon has produced many innovative ideas and become the main event of TechnoScape.

Why You Should Join
Networking
Participants will have the opportunity to interact with other participants, mentors, and also judges of Hackathon 4.0.
Mentoring With Experts
Participants will have the opportunity to interact with mentors regarding business, design, and technology topics. The mentors will give feedback on your product.
Chance of Job Interview
CVs of the Hackathon 4.0 winners will be distributed to the sponsor companies of Hackathon 4.0.
Chance of Job Interview
Hackathon 4.0 is the place for participants to show their skills. Therefore, the winner will have a chance to get a job offer from the company.
Add Value to Your CV
With all the facilities Hackathon 4.0 offers, participants will have the opportunities to develop their skills with mentoring sessions. Moreover, they have to make an innovative product based on a given case.
